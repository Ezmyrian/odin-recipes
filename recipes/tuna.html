Best Tuna Sandwich

Delicious tuna sandwich

This delicious tuna sandwich employs several distinct flavors which meld beautifully together

Ingredients

  • Two cans tuna in water
  • Two to four tbsp mayo or miracle whip
  • One ounce sweet relish
  • One egg, hardboiled and diced
  • Half ounce onion, diced
  • Dash celery seed
  • Sliced bread

Steps

  1. Drain Tuna and scoop into bowl
  2. Stir in mayo, relish, diced onion, and celery seed, and egg
  3. Spread on one slice bread, cover with a second slice, and enjoy
