Cheesecake

Decandantly delicious cheesecake

This decandantly creamy cheesecake is made in an unconventional way. The taste however, will be more than worth the wait. Takes approximately 20-25 minutes to prep, and four hours to chill.

Ingredients

  • One box (11 oz) vanilla wafers
  • Two ounces milk chocolate chips
  • Two sticks butter
  • Eight ounce can of sweetened condensed milk
  • Eight ounces cream cheese
  • Third of a cup lemon juice
  • One tsp vanilla extract
  • One cup whipped topping
  • Toppings to suit (optional)

Steps

  1. Allow cream cheese and whipped topping to thaw
  2. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  3. Melt butter and chocolate in saucepan over low heat
  4. Crush vanilla wafers into small crumbs using a food processor or rolling pin
  5. Pour vanilla wafer crumbs into a bowl and add the butter/chocolate mixture once melted, mixing thoroughly
  6. Spray 9x9 pan with nonstick spray or coat lightly with butter or shortening
  7. Spread vanilla wafer mixture around bottom and sides to form crust
  8. Bake crust for fifteen minutes and then let cool
  9. In medium bowl mix cream cheese, condensed milk, lemon juice, and vanilla extract with blender
  10. Add whipped topping and gently fold in by hand with a spoon
  11. Pour cream cheese mixture into crust and chill for four hours
  12. Add toppings as desired and enjoy
