Homemade Spaghetti with Parmesan

This homemade spaghetti recipe is sure to delight. While it takes considerable time to cook it is well worth the investment.

Ingredients

  • One pound hamburger meat
  • One package spaghetti noodles, angel hair recommended
  • Bell pepper half, diced
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Newman's Own Sockarooni spaghetti sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Fresh Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. In medium skillet brown hamburger meat. Drain
  2. Add bell pepper and mushrooms
  3. Add garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste
  4. Mix under medium-low heat until meat begins to sear
  5. Add spaghetti sauce and mix
  6. Cover and let simmer, stirring occassionally to prevent burning while letting sauce thicken
  7. In large pot bring one gallon of water to a rolling boil
  8. Add pasta to water and cook per the pasta directions to desired firmness
  9. Place desired amount of pasta on plate, top with spaghetti meat, and flavor with Parmesan
